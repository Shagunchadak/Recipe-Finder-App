Chicken Curry

Enjoy a flavorful and spicy chicken curry perfect for a family meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add all spices and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Pour in coconut milk, simmer, and cook until the chicken is tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with rice or naan.

Grilled Chicken

A healthy and delicious grilled chicken recipe perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. Rub chicken breasts with olive oil and season with paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place chicken on the grill and cook for 5-7 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  4. Serve with grilled vegetables or a salad.

Chicken Soup

A warm and comforting chicken soup, perfect for cold weather.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, cook chicken in broth until tender.
  2. Add carrots, celery, and onion, and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Add thyme and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer for another 10 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve hot with bread.
