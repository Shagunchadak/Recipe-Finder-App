Grilled Chicken

A simple yet delicious grilled chicken recipe with a smoky flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the chicken breasts with the seasoning mix and grill for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  4. Serve with a squeeze of lemon juice.

Pasta Primavera

A light and refreshing pasta dish loaded with colorful vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic, bell pepper, zucchini, and tomatoes for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Combine the pasta and vegetables, tossing to coat evenly. Sprinkle with Parmesan, salt, and pepper before serving.

Vegetable Stir Fry

A healthy and vibrant vegetable stir fry with a soy-based sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and ginger, and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Add broccoli, bell pepper, snap peas, and carrots. Stir fry for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce and rice vinegar, and stir to coat. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
