Tomato Soup

Classic, comforting tomato soup perfect for any season.

Ingredients:

  • 6 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried basil

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, vegetable broth, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes until tomatoes are tender.
  4. Puree the soup using an immersion blender or regular blender. Stir in heavy cream and serve warm.

Chicken Soup

A hearty and nourishing soup made with tender chicken and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine chicken broth, carrots, celery, and onion. Bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat, add chicken, thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 25-30 minutes.
  3. Remove bay leaf and serve the soup hot.

Vegetable Soup

A flavorful and nutritious vegetable soup that is simple to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, sauté onion, carrots, and celery until soft.
  2. Add zucchini, green beans, vegetable broth, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
