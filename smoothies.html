Mango Smoothie

A tropical and refreshing mango smoothie, perfect for a hot day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Combine mango, yogurt, milk, and honey in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again until chilled.
  4. Serve cold and enjoy!

Berry Smoothie

A sweet and healthy smoothie packed with berries and antioxidants.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup raspberries
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions:

  1. Combine all berries, almond milk, and honey in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Serve chilled and garnish with extra berries if desired.

Green Smoothie

A healthy green smoothie to boost your energy and detox your body.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup coconut water
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Instructions:

  1. Place spinach, banana, pineapple, coconut water, and chia seeds in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing taste!
