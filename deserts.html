Chocolate Cake

Indulge in a rich and moist chocolate cake, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, and baking powder.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Gradually add boiling water and mix until batter is smooth and thin.
  5. Pour into prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes. Let cool before serving.

Cheesecake

A creamy and smooth cheesecake topped with your favorite fruits.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 3 packages (8 oz) cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C). Grease a springform pan.
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press mixture into the pan.
  3. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Pour over crust and bake for 50-60 minutes. Let cool and refrigerate before serving.

Apple Pie

A classic apple pie with a flaky crust and sweet, spiced apples.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 package refrigerated pie crusts
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Prepare pie crust in a pie dish.
  2. Mix apples, sugars, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice in a bowl.
  3. Fill pie crust with apple mixture, dot with butter, and cover with top crust.
  4. Bake for 45-50 minutes, until crust is golden brown. Let cool before serving.
