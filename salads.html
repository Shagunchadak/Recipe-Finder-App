Caesar Salad

A classic Caesar salad with fresh greens, croutons, and creamy dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • Fresh ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, dressing, and parmesan cheese.
  2. Top with croutons and a sprinkle of black pepper.
  3. Toss to combine and serve immediately.

Greek Salad

A refreshing salad with cucumbers, tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar.
  3. Toss gently and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Fruit Salad

A sweet and colorful mix of fresh seasonal fruits.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup watermelon, cubed
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup orange juice

Instructions:

  1. Combine all fruits in a large bowl.
  2. Pour orange juice over the fruit and toss gently.
  3. Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
  4. Enjoy the refreshing fruit salad!
